PRIVACY POLICY
Permissions Tester

Last Updated: 2026-07-17T16:53:42Z

1. Overview
Permissions Tester is an educational and diagnostic tool designed to help developers 
and users understand iOS permission statuses. This app does NOT collect, store, 
transmit, or share any personal data.

2. Data Collection
This app does NOT:
- Collect any location data
- Access or transmit photos or media
- Record audio or video
- Access contacts or calendar information
- Track user behavior
- Use analytics or crash reporting
- Require network connectivity (works entirely offline)

3. Permission Access
When you grant permissions (Camera, Microphone, Photos, Location, etc.), 
the app only checks the permission status and displays it locally on your device. 
No data leaves your device.

4. Third-Party Services
This app does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising.

5. Children's Privacy
This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect 
data from children.

the "Last Updated" date above.
